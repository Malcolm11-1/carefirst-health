About CareFirst

Compassionate healthcare triaging, powered by smart priority scoring

Why CareFirst?

In many healthcare facilities, patients are served on a first-come-first-served basis. This can leave elderly patients and critically ill individuals waiting in pain while less urgent cases are attended to.

CareFirst solves this by automatically computing a priority score based on:

  • Age — patients 65 and above receive higher priority
  • Condition severity — critical and severe cases are escalated
  • Wait time — the longer someone waits, their priority gradually increases

This ensures fair, compassionate, and medically sound triaging at every facility.
